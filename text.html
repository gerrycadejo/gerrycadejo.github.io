You'll need:
1. Pens
2. Post-it notes
3. Blank pieces of paper (yes both post-its and paper)
3. Teamwork!
Instructions
1. The facilitator asks why the problem is happening.
2. The group should discuss and agree on one reason.
3. The facilitator types the reason you all agree with and clicks on ask.
4. Start again from step #1 until the screen tells you to move on.
Example:
Problem: Artists don't get exposure on the site
Why is this happening?
Artists show the wrong info
Why is this happening?
Artists spend little time fixing their profile
Why is this happening?
Artists don't see the value of having a nice profile
