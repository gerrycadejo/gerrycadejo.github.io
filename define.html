RUN DMC20
Instructions
1. Get a piece of paper divide it in three and number each part. (i.e 1, 2 3)
2. You'll have 3 minutes to write down ONE solution for the problem. Use the first part of the paper
3. Share your idea with the group
4. Everybody gives their idea, and the paper, to the person who is to their right
4. Now use the second part of the paper and you'll have 2 minutes to write down what might limit the other person's idea
5. Explain the limitations you saw with the person who is at your right and then give them the paper
6. Now you'll have 3 minutes. Use the third part of the paper and solve the idea's limitations
7. Share your strong ideas with the group*
If unclear, please check the example.

*Note: The objective of this workshop is to use each other as a way to have stronger ideas.

DOTMOCRACY
Decision making process.

1. Make sure everyone has heard the shorlist of ideas.
2. Name each idea.
3. Write it down and put it in a place where everyone can see it.
4. Everyone should add a small dot on the idea they prefer. Every person gets 3 dots to give.
5. In case there is a tie, do it again with the ones who had the most dots.
6. In case there are still tied, pick one randomly and use the other idea later(you can acess the wind stage directly).
