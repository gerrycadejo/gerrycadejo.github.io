HOLA

1

I used to write plays when I was in high school. I remember writing for hours, reading it out loud, editing, laughing and then fantasizing about the opening show. Even though, me and my plays got butchered everytime by the school critique, it was really fun and in a way, these were my firsts tries as a writer.

Back then, all I was certain of was that I liked going stupid and me and my highschool sweetheart were gonna stay together forever. I was 17 at the time, the world was mine and failure was not even on my radar.

I was all about the fancy job titles, academic achievements and serious adults matters. Now, I see I was just a boy, completely unaware that 10+ years later I was gonna trade the academic life for free-spirited experiences and have "telling stories" as one of my main skills.

I couldn't have possibly guessed that a day like today I would call myself a creative and do serious adult kind of work without losing my own stupid touch. For example Apple TV Bites.
